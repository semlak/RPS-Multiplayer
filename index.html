App Help
  • This is a Multi-player Rock-Paper-Scissors game that lets users play against each other over the internet, in a web browser, written primarily in JavaScript.
  • To Play:
    1. Login or Sign Up (click Login at top-right of screen). Authentication is handled with Firebase, so you will need a valid email address and a password of at least 6 characters.
    2. After logging in, if you were in the middle of an active came, it should reload. If not, click on one of the available users (in box "Other Available Users") to start a new game.
    3. For each play, select Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The game should then wait until your opponent makes a play, the app determines who wins that round, and the score is updated.
    4. After enough games are won or lost (best 3 out of 5, by default), the players are notified and the game is over. You can play again.
  • View the project's GitHub page at "https://github.com/semlak/RPS-Multiplayer"
